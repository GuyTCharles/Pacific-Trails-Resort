Activities at Pacific Trails Resort

Hiking

We offer ten miles of scenic hiking trails along a beautiful state park. Explore on your own or join a guided hike.

Mountain Biking

Explore designated biking trails through scenic forest paths and rolling terrain. Perfect for both beginners and experienced riders.

Bird Watching

While anytime is a good time for bird watching at Pacific Trails Resort, we offer guided birdwatching trips at sunrise several times a week.

Kayaking

Ocean kayaks are available for guest use, allowing you to glide across the gentle waves and discover breathtaking coastal views from a unique perspective.

Fishing

Spend a peaceful morning fishing along the river or nearby lake. Equipment rentals and guided fishing experiences can be available for guests.

Campfire Evenings

Gather around a cozy campfire at sunset. Enjoy storytelling, marshmallow roasting, and relaxing under the stars with family and friends.
