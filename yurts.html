Discover Luxury Yurts at Pacific Trails

What is a yurt?
Our premium yurts are stylish, permanent retreats elevated four feet above the ground. Each offers durable canvas walls, a beautiful wooden floor, and an airy roof dome you can open to the sky.
How are the yurts furnished?
Enjoy a queen-size bed with a plush down quilt, a cozy gas fireplace, full electricity, and a private sink with hot and cold running water. Modern shower and restroom facilities are conveniently located in the lodge.
What should I bring?
Bring your adventurous spirit and take time to unwind. We recommend packing comfortable walking shoes and versatile clothing to adapt to the ever-changing weather.
